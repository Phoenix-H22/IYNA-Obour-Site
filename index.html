ABOUT US

IYNA chapter is part of the global IYNA, an international platform which inspires and educates high school students about neuroscience and the brain. IYNA Obour STEM chapter is founded in July at Egypt. We are a group of young neuroscientists who are passionately dedicated to help the next generation of neuroscientists, hoping to achieve a better understanding of neuroscience through sessions, competitions, events, and courses.
