Our Story

History

Founded: 2016

The IYNA was founded on March 20, 2016 by a group of competitors at the USA National Brain Bee in collaboration with Dr. Norbert Myslinski. Initially called the Youth Neuroscience Clubs of America (YNCA), this organization hoped to connect high school students interested in neuroscience through a network of clubs.
